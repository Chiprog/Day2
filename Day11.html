GRID LAYOUT

This ia a property of the display element and is used to arrange the layout if your work.
Grid layout arranges your work in the form of grids.
The specific dimension of each grid can be specified.
It is most useful when designing news paper website and large screens for orderliness and grouping.
It also makes it easier to design pages without using float and position.
